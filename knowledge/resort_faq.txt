=== BIG BROS WHITE SAND RESORT — KUYA'S KNOWLEDGE BASE ===
 
RESORT OVERVIEW
Big Bros White Sand Resort is a boutique mountain resort in Zambales, Philippines.
Includes two soaking pools, the larger adult pool is 5 feet deep. The smaller kid pool is 3 feet deep.
Intimate, family-friendly, white sand beach vibes with many shell decorations complete with a gorgeous mountain view. Not a mega-resort.
This is personal, relaxed, like staying at a friend's beach house except nicer.
Two luxury suites and a private event space. The resort can also be booked as a
full private buyout for weddings, corporate retreats, and celebrations.

KUYA'S LOCAL KNOWLEDGE
Best sunset viewing spot and time? From the event area at around 5:30pm... Have a drink and watch the sunset.
Nearby attractions — Hiking and river swimming in Cawag. Many decent beaches near Subic bay. Both are roughly 15 minute drive.
Local restaurants worth recommending: Taramen has the best ramen in town. Many samgyupsal places offer unlimited food.
What to bring: Sunscreen, cash for local vendors, a sense of adventure.
Best months to visit: November to May. Rainy season: June to October.
